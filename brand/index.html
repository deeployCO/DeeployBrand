01

Logo

The Deeploy mark pairs a wordmark in Avenir Black with a staircase of descending squares — a visual metaphor for structured deployment and forward momentum. Always use supplied SVG files.

Primary

Red mark + Navy wordmark. Default usage on white and light surfaces.

Reversed

Full white. For dark backgrounds, navy sections, photography overlays.

Monochrome

Blue King only. For print, stamps, and photocopies where color is unavailable.

On photography

White version on cinematic photo overlays. Minimum 50% dark overlay on image.

Minimum sizes

Wordmark: 120px wide digital, 30mm print. Mark: 40px digital, 10mm print.

Clear space

Maintain clear space equal to the height of the square mark on all four sides.

Never distort

Do not stretch, skew, rotate, or alter proportions. Always scale uniformly.

Never recolor

Only approved variants: Primary, White, Monochrome. No custom colors or partial recoloring.

Never add effects

No shadows, glows, outlines, or gradients. The SVG file is the source of truth.

No logo on red

Never place the red primary logo on a red or pink background. Use white version instead.
